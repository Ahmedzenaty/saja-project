History Of Our Pet Planet Store

In 1998, The owner of the shop J.K. Symancyk had a kitten named Alfredo, and he had to travel a very long distance to buy supplies and food for this kitten, but he wasn't the only one who had to do that. As more than half of all households in America have at least one pet.
